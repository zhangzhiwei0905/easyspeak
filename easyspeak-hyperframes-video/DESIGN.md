# EasySpeak Video Design

## Style Prompt

Create a warm, polished product explainer for a WeChat Mini Program that helps users practice spoken English every day. The visual language should feel like a calm morning study desk translated into clean app UI: soft paper backgrounds, purple-blue brand motion, crisp phone mockups, conversational English snippets, progress signals, and lightweight data cards. Keep it practical and app-like, not a generic SaaS launch trailer.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#f6f4ff`
- Ink: `#1a1a2e`
- Muted ink: `#626071`
- Card: `#fffefe`
- Primary: `#667eea`
- Primary dark: `#764ba2`
- Accent: `#e8590c`
- Success: `#52c41a`
- Soft line: `#e8e5f7`

## Typography

- Use system UI fonts: `-apple-system`, `BlinkMacSystemFont`, `Segoe UI`, `Helvetica Neue`, `Arial`, sans-serif.
- Headlines are confident and compact, using 700-800 weight.
- Body and captions are readable and conversational, using 400-600 weight.

## Motion

- Use smooth product motion: slide, rise, count-in, card stacking, progress fills, and gentle phone rotations.
- Entrances should feel quick but not frantic, roughly 0.35-0.7 seconds.
- Background decoratives should drift slowly with finite repeats.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use dark cyberpunk, neon dashboards, or heavy cinematic effects.
- Do not make the project feel like a generic language app; show the specific daily content, learning, review, and quiz loop.
- Do not rely on stock imagery or fake people.
- Do not overuse purple-blue gradients; keep the brand colors as accents and product surfaces as the main signal.
